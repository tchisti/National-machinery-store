The Plastic Oil Filter is lightweight, fully hydraulic, and is the best choice for new oil mill plants. Available in all sizes at a reasonable price. The Casting Oil Filter is heavy-duty cast iron — more durable for high-volume operations. Both are available and we help you select the right one for your setup and budget.

The Vassal Oil Neutralizer is used in the oil refining process to neutralize free fatty acids (FFAs) in crude edible oil, improving oil quality and shelf life. It is a key component of the post-extraction refining stage in any edible oil plant.

A Radler (Redler) Chain is used in en-masse conveying systems for bulk materials like oilseeds, cake, flour, and granules. In oil mills, it moves material through enclosed troughs without breakage or segregation — a critical internal material handling component.
